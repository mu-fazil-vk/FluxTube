<p><i>FluxTube</i> is a powerful and ad-free YouTube client built using Flutter. Watch YouTube videos without ads, subscribe to channels, retrieve video dislikes, read comments, save videos, and much more.</p><p><br><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li><b>No Login Needed:</b> Use the app without any login requirements.</li><li><b>Ad-Free Experience:</b> Enjoy YouTube videos without interruptions.</li><li><b>Channel Subscriptions:</b> Subscribe to your favorite channels.</li><li><b>Dislike Retrieval:</b> See the number of dislikes on videos.</li><li><b>Comment Section:</b> Read the video comments.</li><li><b>Save Videos:</b> Save videos to watch later.</li><li><b>Select Your Region:</b> Customize the content based on your region.</li><li><b>Multi-Language Support:</b> Use the app in multiple languages.</li><li><b>Watch live streams:</b> Effortlessly enjoy live content.</li><li><b>Different Audio Selection Support:</b> Choose from different audio options.</li><li><b>Watch Videos up to 4K Quality:</b> Enjoy videos in high quality up to 4K resolution.</li></ul>
